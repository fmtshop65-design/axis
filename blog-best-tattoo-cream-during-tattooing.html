Best Tattoo Cream to Use During Tattooing

Why a professional working glide matters, how AXIS Silver Butter is formulated, and how to use it correctly during your sessions.

← Back to Blog

Why Use a Tattoo Working Glide?

A tattoo working glide (also called a tattoo cream or barrier cream) is applied to the skin surface during a session. It serves three critical functions:

  • Lubrication: Reduces friction between the needle and skin, allowing smoother, faster passes with less skin disturbance.
  • Skin protection: Creates a thin film that limits micro-abrasion, reduces redness, and keeps the work surface clean and visible.
  • Client comfort: Ingredients like Clove Extract provide mild analgesic action, reducing perceived discomfort during longer sessions.

AXIS Silver Butter — Formulation Breakdown

Silver Butter is AXIS's professional working glide, developed specifically for use during the tattoo process.

Key Active Ingredients

  • Cannabis Sativa Seed Oil: Rich in omega-6 and omega-3 fatty acids. Proven anti-inflammatory action reduces swelling, redness, and skin stress during intensive sessions.
  • Clove Extract: Natural eugenol content provides topical analgesic and antimicrobial properties — soothing the client and keeping the work area clean.
  • Coconut & Mango Blend: Signature exotic scent. Natural moisturising properties prevent skin drying out during extended sessions.

How to Use Silver Butter

  • Before the session: Apply a thin layer to shaved, clean skin. Wait 1–2 minutes before beginning.
  • During the session: Reapply as needed — especially when switching needle configurations or after wiping.
  • Amount: Less is more. A thin, even film is sufficient. Excess product obscures the design stencil.

Pro tip: Apply Silver Butter before and immediately after removing the stencil film. This preserves stencil clarity while conditioning the skin for the first passes.

Silver Butter vs. Vaseline

Petroleum jelly (Vaseline) has been used in tattoo studios for decades — but it is a passive barrier only. It provides no active anti-inflammatory or analgesic benefit. AXIS Silver Butter is purpose-formulated for the tattooing process, with active ingredients that reduce skin trauma and improve the overall session experience for both artist and client.

Available Sizes

  • 150ml — Travel Size: ideal for guest spots, conventions, and guest artists.
  • 250ml — Standard: the most popular studio jar for daily professional use.
  • 350ml — Professional: maximum value for high-volume studios.
