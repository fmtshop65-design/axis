Silver Balm AXIS: The Ultimate Professional After-Tattoo Care Balm

What happens to a tattoo in the 14 days after the session determines how it heals for a lifetime. Silver Balm AXIS was formulated specifically for that window.

← Back to Blog

Why After-Care Is Half the Tattoo

A flawless session can be undone by poor healing. Ink migration, premature scabbing, colour loss, and uneven texture are almost never the artist's fault — they are the result of inadequate after-care in the first two weeks. The skin has been punctured thousands of times. It needs a product that seals, nourishes, and lets the healing process run without interference.

Most generic balms and petroleum-based products do the opposite: they suffocate the skin, trap bacteria, and slow cellular recovery. Silver Balm AXIS was developed to solve that.

The Formula: What Makes It Different

  • Sativa Seed Oil: The core active ingredient. Rich in omega-3 and omega-6 fatty acids in an ideal3:1 ratio that mirrors the skin's own lipid profile. It penetrates without clogging pores, accelerates tissue regeneration, and reduces post-session inflammation.
  • Butter-Soft Texture: Not a liquid, not a thick cream. Silver Balm melts on contact with skin temperature, delivering an ultra-thin, even layer that coats the tattooed area without excess buildup. No sticky residue. No transfer onto clothing or bedding.
  • Coconut & Mango Scent: A light, natural fragrance from the source ingredients themselves — not added perfume. Suitable for clients with fragrance sensitivities and will not interfere with the smell of fresh ink during a session.
  • Non-Occlusive Barrier: Protects the wound surface while still allowing the skin to breathe. This is critical for preventing over-moisturisation, which causes premature scab softening and ink loss.

Professional note: Silver Balm AXIS contains no lanolin, no petroleum derivatives, and no artificial preservatives. It is safe to use from day one — directly after removing the initial wrap.

How to Use Silver Balm AXIS

The application protocol is simple enough that clients follow it consistently — which is exactly the point. Complicated routines get skipped.

  • Day 1–3(Acute phase): Gently clean the tattoo with lukewarm water and unscented soap. Pat completely dry. Apply a thin layer of Silver Balm — the size of a pea for a palm-sized piece —3times per day. Do not rub in. Let it absorb.
  • Day 4–7 (Peeling phase): The skin will begin to peel. Do not pick. Apply Silver Balm twice daily. The sativa oil supports the natural exfoliation process and keeps the new skin supple.
  • Day 8–14 (Settling phase): Apply once daily or as needed. The ink is settling into the dermis. Consistent moisture during this phase preserves vibrancy and edge sharpness long-term.

For the Artist: Why It Belongs in Your Studio

Silver Balm AXIS is not only a retail product — it is a professional tool. Artists who apply it immediately after finishing the session, before wrapping, report significantly reduced redness and swelling in session photos taken even30 minutes post-work. The anti-inflammatory action of sativa seed oil is rapid.

  • Stencil-phase use: A micro-thin layer during long sessions helps the skin stay calm between passes on large-scale work.
  • Client handout protocol: Include a sample with every booking confirmation. Clients who start care correctly from day one come back for touch-ups less frequently — and refer more clients.
  • Pairs with AXIS cartridges: Because AXIS cartridges are membrane-equipped and minimize skin trauma per pass, Silver Balm's regeneration formula has less inflammation to counter and works faster.

Artist tip: Keep Silver Balm at the station. A thin application immediately after wiping the finished piece — before photographing — makes the work photograph significantly better. The sativa oil restores the skin's natural sheen without the artificial gloss of petroleum jelly.

Professional Care Standards

The tattoo industry has long treated after-care as the client's responsibility. The artists who are building long-term reputations understand that it is a shared responsibility. Recommending — or providing — Silver Balm AXIS is a statement about your standards. It tells the client that the work matters past the moment they leave the studio.

Every batch of Silver Balm AXIS is produced under controlled cosmetic manufacturing standards. The formula is dermatologist-reviewed, and the packaging is designed for hygienic single-touch application at the studio and at home.
