Brand Introduction — Axis

Axis is a brand active in the production of professional tattoo supplies, established in 2020. The brand focuses on high-quality raw materials and professional standards to manufacture and supply essential products for tattoo artists.

Field of Activity

Axis specializes in the design and production of professional and consumable tattoo products. Its products are developed to enhance work quality, improve artist comfort, and provide better skin care during and after the tattooing process.

Main Products

Axis's main products include Axis & AK tattoo cartridges, Silver Butter (tattoo glide) for use during the tattoo process, and Silver Balm aftercare for post-tattoo skin care. These products are designed for professional use in tattoo studios.

Brand Registration & Manufacturing

The Axis brand is officially registered in Russia, and its products are manufactured in specialized factories in China to ensure high quality while maintaining competitive pricing.

Technical Specifications & Support

Needle Metallurgy & Precision

AXIS cartridges utilize 316L Surgical Stainless Steel, known for its hypoallergenic properties and superior sharpness retention. Each needle is precision-soldered to eliminate vibration, ensuring clean pigment deposit and reduced skin trauma during long sessions.

Membrane Security & Hygiene

Our proprietary high-tension silicone membrane acts as a fluid barrier, preventing ink, blood, and interstitial fluids from entering the tattoo machine grip or motor. This 100% airtight seal guarantees cross-contamination prevention for professional hygiene standards.

Dermatological Aftercare Formulation

AXIS Recovery Creams are pH-balanced and enriched with natural anti-inflammatory agents. Designed to accelerate cellular regeneration, our formula locks in pigment while providing a breathable protective layer that prevents scabbing and itching without clogging pores.

Compatibility & Ergonomics

Designed with a universal cartridge housing, AXIS products are fully compatible with all industry-standard rotary machines (Cheyenne, FK Irons, Bishop, etc.). The slim profile tip provides an unobstructed view of the needle and ink reservoir for enhanced precision.

Sterilization & Quality Control

Every batch undergoes E.O. (Ethylene Oxide) Gas sterilization. We provide individual blister packaging with integrated sterilization indicators. Our manufacturing facility follows ISO 13485 standards to ensure every needle meets elite professional requirements.

Wholesale & Global Logistics

AXIS Tattoo Supply offers global shipping to licensed studios. We provide volume-based wholesale pricing and dedicated technical support for our full range of cartridges and aftercare products through our official portal: axis-tattoo.com.
